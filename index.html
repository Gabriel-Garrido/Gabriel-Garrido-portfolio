About

Gabriel-Garrido, from Santiago, Chile.

My name is Gabriel, and I am a nurse with a passion for technology and healthcare. I have been working in primary health care since 2018, where I have had the opportunity to develop a wide range of skills, including team leadership, data analysis, strategy implementation, and clinical care. As I continued working in healthcare, I realized the importance of technology in transforming the industry. I wanted to learn more about how to leverage technology to optimize nursing care processes and improve care management. However, as a nurse, I found that I lacked the IT knowledge necessary to keep up with the ever-changing healthcare landscape. I needed to find a way to gain these skills to be able to lead in digital transformation. In March 2023, I completed the Full Stack Developer bootcamp at 4GeeksAcademy, where I learned the skills needed to stay ahead in healthcare technology. With my newfound knowledge, I can now be a leader in digital transformation and optimize nursing care processes for better patient outcomes.
